- SMI: Abbrev. for Signs of Mental Illness.

- I've marked most of the interactable objects with the color 'royalblue'. See the left!
- It may takes a few while to load data. Your patience will be greatly appreciated!
- The data size is rather small so you may not want to use too many filters.
